A one-stop spot for a plethora of home-style Himalayan preparations from Tibet, Nepal, Bhutan and North-East India. Yeti’s exotic array of delicacies includes Thakali Thali, Gyuma, Choila, Thukpa, and other Nepali, Bhutanese and Tibetan snacks.

OUR OFFERINGS

Treat yourself to a luxurious experience with exotic regional offerings from the Himalayas. The interior design of the restaurant is based on Himalayan culture and tradition. We offer a relaxing ambiance with dishes based on original recipes prepared with spices from the Himalayan regions.
